Hello!

I'm a South Carolina risen UI/UX designer, iOS Developer, now planted in Dallas, Texas. I graduated from Full Sail University with a Bachelor of Science in Mobile Development, and have been designing and coding iOS applications for several years now.

I have worked on enterprise level applications for Barclays Capital and have even designed, developed and released my own personal applications to the App Store. Since the beginning of starting mobile development, and even during college, I have spent my spare minutes focusing on UI/UX design and writing code, to stay up-to-date, learning all I can to keep up with the constant change of mobile technology. I aim to make the users lives a little easier, by designing and developing software that is intuitive and meaningful.
